#=-------------------------------=#
# Goals for Creating a Menu Sytem #
#=-------------------------------=#

	- The menu should:
		- be a self contained.
		- be well documented.
		- clearly written.
		- be able to change between menues.
		- be able to load the world scene.
		- handle sub-menues.
	- Nothing should break if the system is 
		- removed.
		- added.
